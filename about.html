History of Lissana

LISSANA is a sewing company that was founded in 2000 in Belarus, Brest. The enterprise is famous for its unique fashion style and high quality fabrics. The company has its own sewing equipment, 3D printers and a laboratory where designers and seamstresses create beauty and fashion.The assortment of LISSANA is based on classic office types of clothes (suits, dresses, skirts, trousers,etc),casual and elegant suits.

The company started its work at the times when fashion industry was not sufficiently developed in the CIS. LISSANA successfully cooperates with and supplies the clothes to Russia,Kazakhstan and Ukraine. Our mission is to expand our production in other markets of Europe and America.
